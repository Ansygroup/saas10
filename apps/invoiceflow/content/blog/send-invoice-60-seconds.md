# How to Send a Professional Invoice in 60 Seconds

You don't need QuickBooks. You need a clean invoice and a reminder. Here's the
exact flow in InvoiceFlow.

## Step 1 — Fill the basics (20s)
Client name, email, amount, due date. Pick a template. Done.

## Step 2 — Turn on auto-reminders (10s)
Toggle "Remind at 7/14/30 days." InvoiceFlow emails the client automatically.
You never touch it again.

## Step 3 — Send (5s)
Hit send. The invoice goes out branded and tracked.

## Step 4 — Watch the dashboard (25s)
Open the dashboard. See what's paid, what's pending, what's overdue — across
all clients.

That's it. No spreadsheet, no Sunday night reconciliation.
