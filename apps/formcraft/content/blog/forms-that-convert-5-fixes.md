# Forms That Convert: 5 Fixes

Your form converts at 2%? Here are the 5 changes that fix most of it.

1. **Shorter** — every extra field drops conversion.
2. **Mobile-first** — 60% fill on phones.
3. **Clear CTA** — "Get my guide" beats "Submit".
4. **Thank-you page** — confirm success, offer next step.
5. **No captcha wall** — use honeypot instead.
