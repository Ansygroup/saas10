# UTM Tags Explained Like You're 5

UTM tags are labels you put on a link so you know where traffic came from.
`?utm_source=twitter` = "this click came from Twitter."

## The 5 tags
- **source** — where (twitter, newsletter)
- **medium** — how (social, email)
- **campaign** — what (summer_sale)
- **term** — paid keyword
- **content** — which button

## Why care?
Without UTMs, Google Analytics says "direct." You can't tell what works.
With them, you see "Twitter drove 40% of signups."

LinkTrack adds these automatically. No manual string-building.
