# invoiceflow — LinkedIn Post (B2B)

Subject: Your invoicing process is costing you clients.

Most freelancers and small agencies lose 15-20% of revenue to:
→ Late payments (no follow-up system)
→ Unprofessional invoices (looks like an afterthought)
→ Zero visibility (who paid? who didn't?)

I built InvoiceFlow to fix all three in one place:
✅ Auto reminder emails at 7/14/30 days
✅ Clean templates that look like a $50/mo tool
✅ Dashboard: sent / paid / overdue at a glance

No spreadsheet. No accounting degree. $19/mo.

If you run a small agency or freelance — what's your invoicing stack?
